The Dr. Wonder Team About Us

Dr. Wonder Comics and Collectibles is a family owned and operated business located in the heart of historic and senic Cranbury, New Jersey. Located in the old Cranbury Bookworm building, we aim to continue the legacy of a friendly shop with surprises in evey aisle, but with the added dimension of a whole new medium: comics! Our staff is friendly and ready to help you find that rare issure you're looking for.
